My Poems

Answer

Answer, Answer, Question
For someone like you
And it would be like a shooting star
I would be a reckless gambler
But you know you're not what they think
And I'm willing to bet
There'd be an earthquake if I bet against you
I'll bet all I have on you
In the realm of uncertainty and doubt
You are the answer that I can't live without
Like a puzzle solved, you bring clarity
With you by my side, I find serenity
But it comes down to the final shot
I'd do anything and everything to have got that dream come true
All I tried to do to try not to let it get away
To find out what I was thinking and what I was doing
Who you are to be, who you are to be
That's another question, another answer
Answer, answer, question.
You are to be, you are to be

Dropped

Now I've Dropped Everything
I've fallen in love with you so easily
You don't have to be scared of me
Now I've dropped everything
I've found a girl
For this chance that we share
You don't have to be scared of me
I could see it in the way her eyes shone
Let's have a past, let's kiss goodbye
And as we smoke in the ashtrays
I wonder if we'll ever run on this memory, oh
Oh no, no, no
Let's stop all this screaming
And never ever leave on the past, oh
I don't want to see
The tears that nobody cries
I don't want to feel the joy of love again
Never get to feel my loneliness
I just can't seem to get much closer
Then I had hoped

Last Journey

In the shoes of my grandfather, I stand,
Seeking solace in words, guided by his hand.
Through these verses, I strive to comprehend,
The journey he undertook, until his life's end.

A grand symphony of life once did he weave,
Full of vigor, strength, and passions to believe.
Yet fate's cruel hand struck him with despair,
An accident, a battle he fought, unfair.

Each day a struggle, a test of his might,
Challenged by shadows that loomed in the night.
Yet his spirit remained unbroken and strong,
For love and family, he carried along.

With every word he penned, every story told,
His legacy lives on, a treasure to behold.
Through these verses, his memory survives,
His last journey complete, as his spirit thrives.

Lost in Time

In the depths of history, I find my way,
Lost in time, where past and present sway.
Ancient ruins whisper tales of old,
Stories of love, valor, and secrets untold.

Through the corridors of time, I wander,
A time traveler, seeking moments to ponder.
I walk the paths once walked by those before,
Imagining their lives, their triumphs, and more.

The crumbling walls echo with the past,
Each brick a memory, destined to last.
I feel the weight of time upon my soul,
As I delve deeper, seeking stories untold.

The whispers of the ancients fill the air,
A symphony of voices, heard everywhere.
Lost in time, I am connected to the past,
A timeless journey, an experience to last.

Whispers of the Sea

I stand upon the sandy shore,
As waves crash and gently roar.
The salty air, a kiss so sweet,
A symphony of whispers, the sea's heartbeat.

I listen closely to the murmurs low,
Secrets of the ocean, it wants to bestow.
Tales of ancient seafarers and lost treasure,
Of love and longing beyond measure.

The waves embrace my weary feet,
Their touch, a comfort, so bittersweet.
They carry away my worries and fears,
And in return, they gift me with tears.

With each crashing wave, a cleansing release,
A moment of solace, a sense of peace.
The whispers of the sea, a soothing song,
Filling my heart, where I belong.

City Lights

In the heart of the city, where dreams collide,
Neon lights shimmer, painting the night sky.
A symphony of car horns and bustling feet,
The rhythm of life, a pulsating beat.

Skyscrapers soar, reaching for the stars,
Casting their glow, like cosmic memoirs.
Streets alive with stories, untold and unseen,
A tapestry of lives, woven in between.

Faces pass by, strangers in the crowd,
Each with a tale, silent and proud.
From all walks of life, they converge and depart,
In this city of dreams, a place to restart.

Amidst the chaos, I find solace and peace,
A sanctuary of inspiration, where worries cease.
For within these city lights, I am free,
To chase my dreams, to be who I want to be.
